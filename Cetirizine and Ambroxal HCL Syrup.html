Cetirizine & Ambroxal HCL

SYRUP PRESENTATION:
Each 5 ml Contains:
Ambroxol 30 mg
Cetrizine Hydrochloride 2.5 mg

This combination of Ambroxal (expectorant) and cetirizine (antihistamine)are used to treat Various allergic conditions.

  • Seasonal and perennial allergies
  • As decongestant

Ambroxal is a mucolytic agent. Nitric oxide enhances the activation of soluble guanylate cyclase and cGMP accumulation. Ambroxal inhibits the activation of NO-dependent guanylate cyclase thus this results in suppression of the excessive mucus secretion.

Cetirizine D1 HCL mainly blocks the H1 receptors presents on the surface of the mast cells and prevent the release of the histamine and block the histamine mediated action

  • Blurred vision
  • Sore throat
  • Stomach pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Drowsiness
  • Vomiting
  • Gastric irritations

  • Contraindicated in pregnancy
  • Contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity

With alcohol
