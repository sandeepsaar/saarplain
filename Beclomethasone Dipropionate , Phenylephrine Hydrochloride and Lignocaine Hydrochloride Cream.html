This composition contains beclomethasone Dipropionate, phenylephrine hydrochloride and lignocaine hydrochloride which are used to provide relief anal fissures and haemorrhoids i.e. piles that cause inflammation and pain. It also stops bleeding.

  • Bacterial sinusitis
  • Asthma
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease
  • Piles

Beclomethasone Dipropionate binds to the glucocorticoid receptors and migrates into nucleus where it binds to glucocorticoid responsive elements and changes in transcription thus prevent the activation of release of inflammatory mediators.
Phenylepinephrine Hydrochloride acts as agonist it mediates vasoconstriction and mydraisis
Lignocaine hydrochloride inhibits the sodium channels so that there is inhibition of nerve impulses thus prevents muscle contraction and causes vasodilation, hypotension and irregular heartbeat.
