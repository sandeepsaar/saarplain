This suspension contains Elemental iron, Mecobalamin, and Folic acid as active ingredients which is used for iron deficiencies in humans useful in anemia caused by low levels of iron or folate in the blood. It is also used in pregnancy for the prevention and treatment of low blood levels of iron and to supply folic acid to the body. This suspension is used mainly for iron deficiency anemia, vitamin B deficiency and other relevant conditions. These deficiencies occur primarily due to illness, pregnancy, poor nutrition, digestive disorders, and many other conditions. Shake the bottle well before consuming this medicine.

  • For treatment and prevention of iron and folic acid deficiency i.e. anemia, characterized by weakness, fatigue, difficulty in breathing, headache, irritability and difficulty in concentration.
  • Treatment and prophylaxis of vitamin and iron deficiency - Anaemia, nutritional supplement in pregnant women and in lactation.

It acts by improving the iron stores and maintaining the optimum level of iron and folic acid in the body. Iron and folic acid are also known as iron replacement products.

  • Ferrous ascorbate is an iron supplement that helps prevent or treat anemia (low blood levels of iron). The ascorbic acid improves the absorption of iron from the stomach into the body.
  • Folic acid plays a vital role in the production of DNA and other genetic material; and also helps in the synthesis of red blood cells.
  • Methylcobalamin a form of Vitamin B12, is a nutrient that helps keep your body's blood and nerve cells healthy and helps make DNA, the genetic material in all of your cells. It also helps prevent megaloblastic anemia, a blood condition that makes people tired and weak
