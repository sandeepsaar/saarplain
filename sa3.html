Ofloxacin:
Ofloxacin is a antibacterial drug belonging to fluoroquinolone group of antibiotics. It is Synthetic chemotherapeutic antibiotic of the fluoroquinolone drug class considered to be a second-generation fluoroquinolone. Ofloxacin is an antibiotic that is used to treat bacterial infections. Ofloxacin is also used for the treatment of typhoid fever, community acquired pneumonia, pelvic inflammatory disease (PID).
Tinidazole:
Tinidazole is an anti-parasitic drug used against protozoan infections. It is widely known throughout Europe and the developing world as treatment for a variety of amoebic and parasitic infections like (giardiasis, amebiasis). Tinidazole is a 5-nitroimidazole with activity against anaerobic bacteria and protozoa.
It has been demonstrated by recent studies concomitant administration of an Ofloxacin (antibiotic) and Tinidazole (anti parasitic) shows significantly better improvement of parasitic infections and pelvic inflammatory disease with each of the treatment.
