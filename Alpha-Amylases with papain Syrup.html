Alpha-amylase is an amylolytic enzyme. It breaks down starch and polysaccharides by attacking the α-1,4-glucosidic linkage at random, yielding dextrins, oligosaccharides and monosaccharides.


Papain is a proteolytic enzyme prepared from unripe fruit of Carica papaya. At body temperature, papain is active over a wide range of pH (pH 5-9). Papain consists of papain and chymopapain which hydrolyses proteins, polypeptides, especially bonds involving basic amino acids or leucine or glycine, yielding peptides.
