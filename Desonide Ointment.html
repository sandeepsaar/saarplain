Desonide Ointment

Indications:-skin



Desonide

OINTMENT PRESENTATION:
Desonide 0.05%

Desonide Ointment is a dermatological preparation indicated for the relief of various skin conditions. It belongs to the class of corticosteroids, which are anti-inflammatory agents.

Indicated for the relief of inflammatory and pruritic manifestations of corticosteroid-responsive dermatoses, such as:

  • Atopic dermatitis (eczema)
  • Contact dermatitis
  • Psoriasis
  • Seborrheic dermatitis
  • Itching and redness associated with various skin conditions

Desonide acts by inhibiting the inflammatory response and suppressing the immune system in the skin. It reduces redness, itching, and swelling associated with various skin conditions.

  • If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, think you may be pregnant or are planning to have a baby, ask your doctor or pharmacist for advice before taking this medicine.
  • Do not use it on the face, groin, or underarms unless directed by your healthcare provider.
  • It is for external use only and should be applied thinly to affected areas of skin
  • Do not apply the ointment to any areas of skin that could be infected.
  • Keep out of reach of the children

Do not use Desonide Ointment if you are allergic to desonide or any components of the ointment.

Common side effects may include mild burning, itching, or irritation at the application site. Notify your healthcare provider if any side effects persist or worsen.
