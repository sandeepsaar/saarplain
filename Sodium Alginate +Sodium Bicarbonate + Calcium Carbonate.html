Sodium Alginate + Sodium Bicarbonate + Calcium Carbonate

SUSPENSION PRESENTATION:
Each 5 ml contains:
Sodium Alginate: 250 mg
Sodium Bicarbonate: 133.5 mg
Calcium Carbonate: 80 mg

This suspension is a combination of three medications. Sodium alginate forms a viscous gel by reacting with stomach acid, where the gel acts as a barrier to reduce reflux. Sodium bicarbonate neutralizes the acidic pH in the stomach. Calcium carbonate decreases the amount of acid in the stomach.

This combination is used for:
Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD):
Used to alleviate symptoms like heartburn and acid regurgitation.
Indigestion:
Relieves symptoms associated with excess stomach acid.

Sodium Alginate:
Forms a protective layer on the stomach contents, preventing reflux into the esophagus.
Sodium Bicarbonate:
Acts as an antacid, neutralizing excess stomach acid.
Calcium Carbonate:
Also functions as an antacid, providing relief from acidity.

Mild and Temporary :- Side effects are generally mild and may include constipation or diarrhea.

Individual Hypersensitivity:- Contraindicated in individuals with known hypersensitivity to any of the components.

Consult a healthcare professional before use during pregnancy or breastfeeding.

Store in a cool, dry place.
