Chlorhexidine, Metronidazole & Lignocaine Gel

Indications:- mouth sores

This composition is used to treat the mouth conditions such as mouth sores and forms the protective covering outside the sores and blocks the pain signal induced.

  • In dental caries
  • As antiseptic
  • In root canal scaling
  • Gingivitis

Chlorhexidine Gluconate-
It is broad spectrum antimicrobial agent also known as biguanide
It is used to as solution to rinse the gums and cavities in gingivitis
It is useful as adjunct in periodontitis and in root canal procedures

Metronidazole Benzoate-
Metronidazole is an antibiotic and belongs to nitroimidazole derivative class. it is effective against parasitic infections.

Lignocaine-
Lignocaine is an anaesthetic and is used to produce the anaesthesia at local or particular side.
Lignocaine has direct depressant effect on the cardiovascular system, beta-adrenergic receptors etc.

Chlorhexidine Gluconate reacts with the phosphate group of the microbial cell surfaces that destroys the integrity of the cell surfaces.

Metronidazole Benzoate binds to the DNA and electron transport proteins and inhibits the synthesis of nucleic acids.

Lignocaine stabilizes the neuronal membrane by inhibiting the ionic fluxes i.e. it blocks the sodium channels located in the internal surface of the membranes of the nerve cells.

  • Dryness in the mouth

Contraindicated in hypersensitivity

  • Avoid use during pregnancy
  • Do not use in conjunctivitis
