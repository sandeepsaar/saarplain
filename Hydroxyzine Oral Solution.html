  • Hydroxyzine is commonly known as disodium citrate
  • It is the salt of the citric acid and is used mainly as urinary alkalinizer
  • It treats the urinary tract infections and renal stones and normalize the PH
  • It is also act as antioxidant

  • Blastomycosis
  • Urinary Acidosis

  • Treat burning micturition
  • Antioxidant
  • In adjuvant therapy with sulphonamides

Hydroxyzine act as buffering agent and neutralizes the excess of the acidic urine as, the acidic urine is more prone to the bacterial infections so therefore it prevents the growth of bacteria.

  • It is absorbed by oral route of administration
  • It is metabolized in the liver
  • It is excreted via kidney
