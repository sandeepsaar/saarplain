Povidone Iodine & Ornidazole Ointment

Indications:- Wound Infections


  • Povidone Iodine & Ornidazole Ointment


OINTMENT PRESENTATION:

Povidone – Iodine IP
(Available Iodine 0.5 % w/w) 5%w/w
Ornidazole 1%w/w Water soluble Ointment base q.s.

This combination is used to treat wound infections here povidone iodine acts as antiseptic by releasing iodine on the infected surface and kills the growth of bacteria whereas ornidazole is an antibiotic it also kills the bacteria.

Temporarily relieves symptoms associated with sinusitis, cold, flu or allergies for the treatment of inflammatory skin conditions such as severe eczema, dermatitis, psoriasis and rash.

Povidone iodine is considered as the active constituent that exerts microbicidal actions. Free iodine releases from the complex and penetrates into the cell wall of microorganisms and damage the protein and nucleic acid and inhibit it synthesis

Ornidazole works by inhibiting the nucleic translational process by releasing the free nitro radical which binds with bacterial protein and thus damage the DNA of microbes.
