DESCRIPTION

Cetrimide cream contains the active ingredients Chlorhexidine gluconate and cetrimide . Cetrimide cream is a topical antiseptic formulation used to prevent and treat infections in minor wounds, cuts, and abrasions. Here’s a brief overview of each component
Chlorhexidine Gluconate (0.06% w/w)

  • Function: Chlorhexidine is a broad-spectrum antiseptic that kills bacteria, viruses, and fungi. It is commonly used for skin infection.
  • Mechanism of action : It disrupts microbial cell membranes, leading to cell death and reducing the risk of infection.

Cetrimide (0.6% w/w)

  • Function: Cetrimide is a surfactant and antiseptic that is effective against a range of bacteria and fungi. It is often used for its cleansing properties.
  • Mechanism: It works by breaking down the cell membranes of microbes, making it effective for both cleaning wounds and preventing infection.

Indications

  • Treating minor cuts, abrasions, and skin irritations.
  • Reducing the risk of infection in small wounds.
  • General skin cleansing in minor surgical procedures.

How to use

  • Do not take this drug by mouth. Use on your skin only.
  • Wash your hands before and after use. If your hand is the treated area, do not wash your hand after use.

Side Effects

Skin Irritation: Redness, itching, or a burning sensation at the application site.


Warning & Precaution

  • For external use only; do not ingest.
  • Avoid contact with eyes, ears, and mouth.
  • If irritation or an allergic reaction occurs, discontinue use and consult a doctor.
  • Use with caution in individuals with sensitive skin.

Interactions

If you are using other creams, ointments, or lotions on the same area, there could be a risk of interaction. It’s generally recommended to avoid applying multiple products simultaneously unless advised by a healthcare provider.


Contraindication

If you are allergic to this drug; any part of this drug; or any other drugs, foods, or substances. Tell your doctor about the allergy and what signs you had.


Storage

Store at room temperature in a dry place. Keep all drugs in a safe place. Keep all drugs out of the reach of children .
