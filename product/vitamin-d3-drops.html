DESCRIPTION

This drops of vitamin D3 helps to get rid of bone disorders such as rickets and osteomalacia in children. As, the age and dark skin leads to restricted exposure to sunlight thus this result in deficiency of vitamin D.


Indications

  • It helps in the metabolism of calcium and phosphorus
  • It helps to build strong bones and teeth’s
  • It promotes the growth and development of kids
  • It enhances the function of immune system

Mechanism

Vitamin D acts on the receptors present in the small intestine which enhances the absorption of calcium and phosphorus. It also stimulates the receptors present in osteoblasts which stimulates the nuclear factor KB which helps the maturation of bones for reabsorption of calcium and phosphorus.


Pharmacokinetics

Absorption: It is absorbed orally from the small intestine

Protein Binding: It is 50-80% bound to proteins

Metabolism: It undergoes hydroxylation and converted into 25-hydroxycalciderol

Excretion: It is excreted via bile and feces


Interactions

  • Phenobarbital
  • Phenytoin
  • Atorvastatin

Side Effects

Common side effects include:

  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Poor appetite
  • Stomach-ache
  • Constipation
  • Diarrhoea

Contraindication

  • Contraindicated in sarcoidosis
  • Contraindicated in Kidney stones
  • Contraindicated in kidney disease
