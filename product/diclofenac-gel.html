DESCRIPTION

This combination is used to relieve the joint pain, stiffness, swelling in arthritis. diclofenac is phenyl acetic acid derivative and non -steroidal anti -inflammatory drug Diclofenac inhibit cox -1 and cox-2 enzyme and prevents the synthesis of prostaglandins.
Linseed oil is known by name flaxseed oil. It is rich in ALA which helps reduction of joint of inflammation. Methyl salicylate: it is the methyl ester of the salicylic acid. It is topically used to provide relief against joint pain caused strains, sprains and arthritis. Methyl salicylate is known as counter irritant. Menthol provides cooling sensation and act as counter irritant. It acts as the activator of the transient receptor potential melastatin compound. Benzyl alcohol has local anaesthetic activity and relieves the superficial skin pain and also has preservative action.


Indications

  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Sprain & strain

Mechanism

Diclofenac - diclofenac is phenyl acetic acid derivative and non -steroidal anti -inflammatory drug Diclofenac inhibit cox -1 and cox-2 enzyme and prevents the synthesis of prostaglandins.
Methyl salicylate - it works by causing the skin to feel cool and warm this helps in reducing pain of joints, muscles and tendons
Menthol - it works by imparting the cooling effect by activating nociceptors and desensitize that receptors and reduces pain.
Linseed oil - flaxseed oil is the source of the polyunsaturated fatty acids. It contains alpha- linolenic acid that decreases the inflammation so therefore it is useful in the rheumatoid arthritis and other inflammatory diseases.

How to use

Apply a drop of gel onto the affected area and rub onto the skin. The amount of gel required will vary depending on the size of the area to be treated. Wash hands after use.
