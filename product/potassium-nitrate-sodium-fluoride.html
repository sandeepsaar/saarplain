DESCRIPTION

This combination is very effective to treat dental problems in the form of toothpaste.

  • Potassium Nitrate: is an inorganic salt and active ingredient in toothpaste and protect the teeths from senstivity against cold and hot , senstivity can be reduce by acting on the intra-dental nerves and inhibit neural transmission.
  • Sodium Fluoride: is an inorganic compound which is the source of the fluoride ion which have many uses like it is used for dental care and in radiographic imaging.

Indications

  • Used to prevent tooth decay.
  • Prevents teeths from dental caries.
  • Sodium fluoride in the oral or topical form is indicated for the prevention and control of dental caries.
  • Treat pain occurs due to increase senstivity.

How to use

  • Do not take this drug by mouth. Use on your skin only.
  • Wash your hands before and after use. If your hand is the treated area, do not wash your hand after use.

Side Effects

The product is possible safe for most adults. There is no disadvantage associated with the product if taken under the recommended usage however mouth/gum irritation may rarely occur.


Warning & Precaution

  • Children younger than 6 years.
  • Do not use this medication for longer than 4 weeks unless directed by your doctor/dentist.
  • Not to be used in pregnancy and breastfeeding.

Interactions

If you are using other creams, ointments, or lotions on the same area, there could be a risk of interaction. It’s generally recommended to avoid applying multiple products simultaneously unless advised by a healthcare provider.


Contraindication

  • sarcoidosis
  • increased activity of the parathyroid gland.
  • high amount of calcium in the blood.
  • dehydration
  • constipation
  • kidney stones
  • decreased kidney function
  • tumor that dissolves bone

Storage

Store at room temperature in a dry place. Keep it in a safe place and out of the reach of children .
