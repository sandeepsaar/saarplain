DESCRIPTION

Levocloperastine Cough Syrup contains the active ingredient Levocloperastine. Levocloperastine is used to treat non-productive cough, also known as dry cough without sputum. Levocloperastine belongs to the antitussive class of medicine. It dissolves the thick mucus formed in the air passages making it easier for you to cough it out. It suppresses the cough by blocking the activity of cough centers in the brain.


Indications

  • Levocloperastine is used in the treatment of Dry Cough.

Interactions

Talk to your doctor or pharmacist before using Levocloperastine Syrup if you are taking other medicines.


Side Effects

  • Sleepiness
  • Dizziness
  • Headache
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea

Special Precautions & Warning

In case of pregnancy or breast-feeding, or when planning to have a baby, it is recommended to ask the doctor for advice before taking this supplement.


Driving and Using Machines

You should be aware of how you react to Levocloperastine Cough Syrup before you drive or operate machinery. If you feel dizzy or drowsy after taking the syrup, do not drive or operate machinery until these effects wear off.


Storage

  • Store at room temperature and out of direct sunlight.
