Indications:- hair loss in men


Minoxidil & Finasteride Topical Solution

TOPICAL SOLUTION PRESENTATION:
Minoxidil IP 5% w/v
Finasteride IP 0.1% w/v

This Solution is a combination medicine used in the treatment of hair loss in men. It works by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles which further prevents hair loss and stimulates re-growth resulting in longer, thicker and increased numbers of hair.
This solution should not be used in women due to the presence of Finasteride, which may cause hormonal changes in females. It is meant for use on the scalp only.

It is used in the treatment of male pattern baldness, premature or hereditary baldness and hair loss (androgenic alopecia) in men. This medicine is recommended for the regrowth of hair in males only.

Minoxidil dilates the blood vessels of the scalp, increases the blood flow to the hair follicles helping in the regrowth of hair
Finasteride works by reducing the production of a hormone called dihydrotestosterone (DHT). In males with hereditary or premature risk for baldness, this hormone DHT is believed to bind to hair follicles and cause them to shrink resulting in hair loss. Thus, both these medicines work together to prevent hair loss and promote regrowth of hair.


Direction for use of:
  • Use this solution only when the scalp or the hair is dry. There is no need to shampoo the hair before or after each application.
  • Use the applicator to fill in 1 ml of the solution and then place it over your head where the hair is thinning and squeeze the bulb of the applicator.
  • Now use your fingertip and spread the solution evenly over the area.
  • Do not squeeze the whole 1 ml at a time.
  • You may apply small amounts and apply thoroughly using the fingertips.
  • Wash your hands after application.
  • Ensure it does not trickle on the face as that can cause unwanted hair growth on the face.
  • Regular hair bath (with mild shampoo) and dyeing of hair need not be discontinued while taking this medicine.

Do not Use if:
  • If you are a women
  • You have no family history of hair loss
  • You do not know the reason for your hair loss
  • Your scalp is red, inflamed, infected, irritated, or painful
  • You use other medicines on the scalp
  • Not be used on babies or for children under 18 years old.

Itching and skin irritation of the treated area of the scalp

Keep in a cool, dry and dark place. Protected from light and heat.
