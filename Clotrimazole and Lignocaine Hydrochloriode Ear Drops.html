This composition contains clotrimazole and lignocaine hydrochloride which are used in combination to treat the fungal infections in the ear. Clotrimazole decreases permeability of membrane and stop the fungal growth whereas lignocaine hydrochloride helps to provide relief from the ear infections.

Clotrimazole belongs to the antimycotic and antifungal class. It falls under the category of imidazole category. It is also used for the treatment of sickle cell anemia and for malaria.
Lignocaine hydrochloride belongs to the category of amide category of local anesthetic. It is indicated for the treatment of ventricular arrhythmias that are life threatening.

Clotrimazole decreased the ergosterol concentration in the fungal cell membrane by inhibiting the 14-alpha demethylase enzyme that is responsible for the conversion of lanosterol into ergosterol thus this prevents the fungal growth.
Lignocaine Hydrochloride works by blocking the sodium channels and thus inhibits the influx of ions thus prevents the induction and conduction of nerve impulses, decreases muscle contraction.
