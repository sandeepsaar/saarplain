Hydroquinone, Tertinoin, Mometasone Furoate Cream

DESCRIPTION

Hydroquinone:
Hydroquinone acts as a strong inhibitor of production of melanin, suggesting that it will prevent skin from making the substance responsible for its colour. Its components have appreciable antioxidant abilities. Hydroquinone, however, does not bleach the skin but makes it light, and can only upset the mixture and fabrication of melanin hyper pigmentation.
Tertinoin:
Tertinoin is used to improve the appearance of the skin by reducing fine lines and wrinkles, reducing roughness, and improving skin color. It belongs to a class of medications called retinoid. It works by affecting the growth of skin cells.
Mometasone:
Mometasone is a cortisone-like steroid which is used to treat certain itchy rashes and other inflammatory skin conditions.

INDICATION

This medication is used to treat a variety of skin conditions (e.g., eczema, dermatitis, allergies, rash)., acne, wrinkles and skin spot such as freckles and dark spots of the skin.

DOSAGE

Apply a thin film of the cream or ointment to the affected skin once a day. Massage it in until it disappears.

SIDE EFFECT

Do not use this Cream if you have ever had an allergic reaction to it or any other steroid medication.
