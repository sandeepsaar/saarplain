Ambroxal, Levocetirizine & Combination Syrup

SYRUP PRESENTATION:
Each 5 ml contains:
Ambroxal HCL IP 15 mg
Levocetirizine HCL IP 0.8 mg
Guaiphenesin IP 50 mg
Phenylephrine HCL IP 5 mg

This combination is used is to treat the chronic allergic conditions such as chronic sinusitis, rhinitis, fever, sore throat, wheezing, cough.elief of dryness or minor irritation of the mouth and throat

  • Cough
  • Asthma
  • Chest Tightness
  • Seasonal allergy
  • Perennial allergy

Ambroxal is a mucolytic agent. Nitric oxide activates the guanylate cyclase and causes accumulation of the cGMP. Ambroxal inhibits the activation of NO-dependent guanylate cyclase which causes the suppression of excessive mucus secretion.
Levocetirizine mainly blocks the H1 receptors presents on the surface of the mast cells and prevent the release of the histamine and block the histamine mediated action
Guaifenesin is as an expectorant that acts by enhancing the expulsion of phlegm(sputum) and bronchial secretions via decreasing the adhesiveness and surface tension of sputum to the respiratory tract
Phenylephrine is sympathomimetic drug and mimics the actions of epinephrine (commonly known as adrenaline) or norepinephrine. Phenylephrine selectively binds to alpha-1 receptors which leads to blood vessels to constrict leads to nasal decongestant action.
