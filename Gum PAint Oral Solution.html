This product contains all that essential ingredients which is used to treat the gum and tooth problems whether it would be bleeding of gums, oral cancer, plaque, dental carries etc. This product treats all these diseases.

  • Painful gum disorders
  • Inflamed and bleeding gums
  • Teething pain and irritation
  • Stomatitis (inflammation of the mucous lining of the mouth)

Iodine acts as antiseptic and anti-infective. Iodine has powerful bactericidal action, quickly gets to work killing bacteria and viruses. Tannic acid acts as astringent for the mucous membrane of the mouth. It forms a film of protein 'tannate' that acts as mechanical protective. Menthol acts as local analgesic and gives sensation of coolness which gives some relief from the pain. Camphor acts as mild analgesic. Thymol is a phenolic compound with antiseptic and antibacterial property.

  • Blistering
  • Itching
  • Diarrhoea
  • Fever
  • Nausea
  • Burning of mouth
  • Skin redness

Contraindicated in eczema and allergies.


Store it in room temperature and in dry place.

Do not rinse with water or eat/drink anything for 10 minutes after the application of gum paint.
