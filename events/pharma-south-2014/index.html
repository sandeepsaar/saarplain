Pharmac South 2014

Saar Biotech had participated in PHARMAC SOUTH 2014 held on 18-19 July, 2014. This International Exhibition was jointly organized by the Indian Drug Manufacturers Association (IDMA-TNPSB) and Orbit Exhibitions Pvt. Ltd. It focused on Contract Manufacturers, Pharmaceuticals, Nutraceuticals Formulations, and more in the Medical & Pharmaceutical industry.

The factory operates with experienced and hardworking employees. It is equipped with sophisticated, modern, and quality control equipment. The facility has also been set up to handle the development of formulations for new drugs. Our product range includes Anti-retrovirals, Anti-malarials, Cardiovascular, and Dermatology.

The two-day national exhibition showcased innovative pharmaceutical products and company profiles for high-quality business inquiries. It aimed to attract more buyers for contract manufacturing services from across the country and beyond. Manufacturing and marketing companies, along with pharma manufacturers, gathered to explore outsourcing and innovative pharmaceutical solutions.

This event, themed 'Destination for Innovative and Reliable Healthcare Products,' provided an exceptional opportunity for exhibitors to market their products, build brand awareness, and increase corporate visibility. The occasion attracted participants from pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, intermediates, and API suppliers, along with medical and pharma sector experts. The specialized conference featured seminars and panel discussions over both days.
