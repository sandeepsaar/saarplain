Gentamicin 0.1% w/w

Gentamicin Sulfate is a wide-spectrum antibiotic that provides highly effective topical treatment in primary and secondary bacterial infections of the skin. Gentamicin Sulfate Cream USP, 0.1% may clear infections that have not responded to other topical antibiotic agents
It works by killing bacteria that cause infections. Gentamicin is bactericidal and is a broad-spectrum antibiotic (except against streptococci and anaerobic bacteria).

  • For the treatment of skin and skin structure infections (e.g., cellulitis, burn wound infection).
Note: Gentamicin Sulfate is a bactericidal agent that is not effective against viruses or fungi in skin infections.

  • Skin irritation
  • Skin redness
  • Itching

  • Contraindicated in patients with a known hypersensitivity towards any ingredient of the product.

  • The use of topical antibiotics occasionally allows overgrowth of nonsusceptible organisms, including fungi. If this occurs, or if irritation, sensitization, or superinfection develops, treatment with Gentamicin Sulfate should be discontinued and appropriate therapy instituted.
  • Do not use this medicine is for inflamed skin around the genitals or anus unless specifically advised to do so.
  • Tell your doctor and pharmacist what prescription and non-prescription medications you are taking, including vitamins
